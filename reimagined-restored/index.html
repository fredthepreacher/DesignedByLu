The Experience

Every piece has a story. We help it tell it.

Reimagined & Restored is a home experience rooted in the belief that beauty is not always found in what is new — sometimes it is recovered from what was overlooked, forgotten, or simply waiting for the right eye to see its potential.

Furniture Restoration Breathing new life into pieces with great bones — refinished, reupholstered, and reimagined to feel both timeless and intentional in your space.
Interior Styling Room-by-room design direction that brings warmth, cohesion, and personality to your home without losing what makes it uniquely yours.
Space Transformation Before-and-after projects that take neglected or transitional spaces and restore them to their full, beautiful potential.
Home Revival Consultation Not sure where to begin? An initial consultation that assesses your space, your vision, and creates a restoration roadmap you can implement over time.

Our Approach

Three beliefs that guide every restoration.

01

Great Bones Matter

The most beautiful transformations often start with pieces or spaces that simply needed someone to see past the surface — to recognize the potential already there.

02

Craftsmanship Over Speed

A well-restored piece done slowly and thoughtfully will outlive and outshine a quick surface fix. We take the time to do the work properly, beautifully, permanently.

03

Your Story, Your Space

Every home reflects the people who live in it. Our restorations are not about making a space look like a magazine spread — they are about making it feel like you.
