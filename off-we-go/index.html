The Experience

The world is too beautiful not to see it.

Off We Go is a travel and lifestyle pillar built around one conviction: exploration is not a luxury — it is a way of seeing. When we step into new places, we often find new parts of ourselves. Lu curates experiences that make that discovery both beautiful and intentional.

Curated Destination Guides Thoughtfully compiled travel recommendations — accommodations, experiences, dining, and hidden gems — filtered through the lens of elegance, faith, and purpose.
Retreats & Group Experiences Women’s retreats, couples getaways, and curated group travel experiences designed to reconnect, restore, and inspire at a pace that actually allows you to breathe.
Family & Faith Adventures Travel experiences rooted in shared memory and wonder — helping families explore the world together in ways that are meaningful, manageable, and memorable.
Lifestyle Inspiration Beyond destinations — food, culture, beauty, and the everyday art of living well wherever you are, informed by the places and people that have shaped the Off We Go journey.

Why We Travel

Three reasons Off We Go exists.

01

Rest Is Not Optional

In a world that never stops, the ability to actually step away — to disconnect in order to reconnect — is one of the most important gifts we can give ourselves and the people we love.

02

Wonder Expands the Soul

There is something that happens when you stand somewhere you have never stood before — your perspective shifts, your gratitude grows, and you return home a little larger than you left.

03

Memories Over Things

The most valuable things you can invest in are the experiences that will still matter in twenty years — the trip your children will talk about, the moment that changed everything. Off We Go helps you create those moments.
